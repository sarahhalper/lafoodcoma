Swedish Cake Recipe

Ingredients:
  • Sockerkaka Recipe
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Raspberries
  • Raspberry jam
  • Vanilla Pudding
Directions:
  1. I have included a link to my favporite sockerkaka recipe to follow for starting this cake. First, bake your cake according to instruction and let cool for an hour.
  2. Prepare heavy whipping cream by whipping until stiff peakms form. Create the vanilla pudding according to insttruction as well. I usually use the brand J-ELLO
  3. Lastly to put the cake together: Cute 3 layers of the cake, put vanilla pudding on the first layer, raspberry jam ont he second and cover the entire cake with the whip cream. Decorate how you would like. I use raspberries but you can use anything.

Cucumber & Hummus Snack

Ingredients:
  • Rice Cakes
  • Hummus
  • Cucumber, sliced thinly
  • Feta Cheese
  • Black Pepper & Salt
Directions:
  1. Start with the base of rice cakes and spread a layer of hummus, however much you would like over both pieces.
  2. Slice your cucumbers and layer them on top of the hummus.
  3. Crush up some feta cheese and sprinkle over top along wioth your desired spices.

Thumbprint Cookies Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup unsalted butter softened to room temperature (226g)
  • ⅓ cup sugar (70g)
  • ⅓ cup light brown sugar, tightly packed (70g)
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 ¼ cup all purpose flour (280g)
  • 2 teaspooons cornstarch
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Raspberry Jam
Directions:
  1. Roll about 1 in balls of cookie dough and press down on a pan slightly flattening them. Use a small spoon and push indents in a heart shape into all the dough pieces.
  2. Spoon the raspberry jam into the indents making sure not to overfill too much as it is likely to spill out and become messy.
  3. Bake at 350F for 8 minutes.

Avocado Toast

Ingredients:
  • Favorite Bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Everything Bagel Seasoning
Directions:
  1. Start with scrambling your 2 eggs in a pan cooking to desired texture.
  2. Toast your bread and slice an avocado into pieces within each half.
  3. Spread butte ron your toast to slightly soften it, add your eggs on top, then the sliced avocado and lastly add desired spices. Personally I use Everything Bagel Seasoning.
