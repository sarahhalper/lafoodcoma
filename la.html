Dessert

Randy's Donuts

I have nothing more to say other than you must try these donuts once while you are in Los Angeles. One of my favorite donut shops I have ever tried with large donuts for a cheap price. Not only are these donuts large but they taste like no other. All the fancy donuts in the world couldnt top their simple glazed texas donut if it tried. I possibly have just been lucky with the donuts ive been handed however each donut has been extraordinary. Though the location isn't great, tyhe drive thru is really easy to get through and you can hang out with friends or family on the beach to enjoy each savory bite.
Overall Rating: 9.7/10
