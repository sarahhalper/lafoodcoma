Dessert

Honey & Butter Macarons

If you have not had these macarons, what have you been doing your whole life. These macarons are the most ecstaticly tasting tiny desserts I have ever put my mouth on. Their main basic called Milk and Honey is the perfect amount of sweetness and perfectly baked. The line is usually out the door especially around holidays.
If you have evert tried to bake macarons you would also know how difficult it is to make them good. Honey & Butter has perfected their methods. The only con is that they are slightly expensive but SO worth it.
Overall Rating: 9.6/10
