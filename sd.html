Breakfast

Naked Cafe

The ultimate brunch spot with a great view of the beach in Del Mar.
If you wnat to have a nice breakfast with family or a little pick me up after laying at the beach, this is the perfect spot for you serving healthy (& unhealthy) options making it feel like a home cooked meal. They have a great outdoor seating area and give a great energy feel.
Overall Rating: 8/10

The ultimate brunch spot serving biottomless mimosa's as well as new choices of meals. One of my personal favorites come to be known as the Lemon Poppyseed pancakes and were moist, fluffy and had the perfeect amount of sweetness to lemony taste.
