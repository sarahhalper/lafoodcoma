Our Guide to the Best Food

LA Food Coma Origin

Hi. Welcome to your Southern California Food Guide. My roommate, Ashleigh, and I moved into the dorms at USC in August of 2021. As two girls who loved to eat out and try new places, we wanted a way to record a favorite places and a way to list what we thought was best to worst. Soon enough, we started a food instagram quickly after we moved into our dorms at USC as a way to rate all the places leaving reviews, good and bad, as well as ratings ranging from 2/10 to 10/10. This way we knew where we liked the best and our best friends could receive some fun insight on where they should really be eating. As you can see above, I added a picture of Ashleigh and I at one of our top picks in LA, Joey DTLA. As two natives to southern california, we also leave reviews in Orange County & San Diego. Hopefully, this gives you some insight into where you should be eating and make sure to check out our instagram for more explanations but for now I hope you enjoy.
