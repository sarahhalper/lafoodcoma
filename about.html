Our Guide to the Best Food

LA Food Coma Origin

Welcome to your Southern California Food Guide. If you love food, this is the perfect place for you! My roommate Ashleigh and I are currently attending USC in Los Angeles and when we first met we bonded over wanting to explore new places in LA. As two girls who love to eat out and try new places, we both decided we could record our favorite places to eat and share them with our friends. Soon enough this turned into the creation of our instagram, @lafoodcoma, giving us both an outlet in the midst of stress and school and a way to explore LA through food. In the picture above you can see Ashleigh and I at one of our top picks in LA, Joey DTLA. As two natives to Southern California, we also have eaten at many places in Orange County & San Diego to leave reviews on. Hopefully, this gives you some insight into where you should be eating and make sure to check out our instagram for more!
